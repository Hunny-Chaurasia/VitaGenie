How to Play Gungi

  • Gungi is played on a 9×9 board. Black moves first.
  • Pieces can be stacked up to 3 tiers on a single square.
  • The piece on top determines movement; stacking changes its behaviour.
  • Captured pieces go to your hand and can be dropped onto empty squares.
  • Win by checkmating the opponent's Marshal (帥).
  • Click a piece to select it, then click a highlighted square to move.
  • Click a hand-piece first, then click an empty square to drop it.
