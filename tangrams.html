How to Play Tangrams

  • Select a piece from the tray by clicking it (it will glow green).
  • Click on the board to place it, or drag it directly from the tray.
  • Press R or click Rotate to rotate the selected piece by 45°.
  • Press F or click Flip to mirror the selected piece.
  • Right-click a placed piece to pick it back up.
  • Fit all 7 pieces into the target silhouette outline to solve.
  • Stats are saved locally — no login needed!
